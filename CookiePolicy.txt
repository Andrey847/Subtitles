What about cookies?

Last update: 22.04.2019

What are cookies. 
Cookies are small pieces of text sent by your browser to a website you visit. Cookies are stored in you web browser and allow sites to recognize you and make your next visit easier.

subtitleslearn.com uses cookies when you visit it. Why? 

1. Authentication cookie to store you token after the login. It is used as part of ASP.NET Core framework for authentication functionality.

2. Site analytics cookie. These cookies allow subtitleslearn.com to analyze site usage and improve it functionality.

3. Third-party cookies. In addition to our own cookies, subtitleslearn.com may also use third-parties cookies to report usage statistic of the Site, deliver advertisements on and through the Site, and so on.
